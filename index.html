SODI KROEHLER

Sodi Kroehler is a junior at the the University of Pittsburgh, with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Philosophy. After growing up in and then leaving the religious group known as the Twelve Tribes in order to pursue an education, Sodi has a unique outlook on ethics and personal responsibility. He is most interested in developing AI applications for societal reform and maintenance, as well as providing a basis for future regulation from a more technical as opposed to legal standpoint. Outside of school, he is the Co-Director of IT in the Alpha Kappa Psi business fraternity as well as a member of the university's Computer Science Club.
